## Project Goal

Deliver two solutions for Green Earth Produce:

1. A **website** that meets GEP’s needs and improves their online presence.
1. An **internal system** that meets their requirements for managing day-to-day work.

The project is considered successful when both solutions are delivered and approved by the client.
