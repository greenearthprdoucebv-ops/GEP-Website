## Purpose

This contract formalizes the collaboration between the four team members to complete the Green Earth Produce Project. The goal is to deliver a website that meets GEP’s needs and an internal system that meets their operational requirements, following Agile methodology.

-----

## Roles and Responsibilities

All members will share the following duties:

- **Research & Analysis:** Understand GEP’s current systems and requirements.
- **Design & Development:** Work on the website and internal system.
- **Quality Control:** Peer-review each other’s work before marking tasks as finished.
- **Agile Participation:** Attend stand-ups and sprint meetings (virtual or in-person).

-----

## Quality Standards

- All work must be peer-reviewed before submission.
- Final deliverables must align with the project’s success criteria and GEP’s requirements.

-----

## Conflict Resolution

Disagreements will be resolved through open discussion. If a resolution cannot be reached, the course instructor will mediate.
