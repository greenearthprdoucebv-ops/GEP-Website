Today we had an online meeting where our team focused on contacting schools in the surrounding area. In total, we reached out to approximately **20 schools** to present our project idea.

Unfortunately, none of the schools were willing to collaborate with us. One school even mentioned that they considered the idea unnecessary, since they expect **parents to directly inform the school if a student is sick**.

Because of this feedback, we decided to **shift our focus to a different project**.

We will now work with a company called **GreenHouse Produce**. Eslam has already been in contact with them, and they agreed to collaborate with our team.

### New Project Direction

Our goal will be to improve their **online presence** by developing or enhancing their website, with the following objectives:

Additionally, during today's meeting we also worked on the **project contract** to formalize the collaboration.
