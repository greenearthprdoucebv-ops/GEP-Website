# Second Interview Questions (Delly) — Green Earth Produce (Problem Definition & Scope)

**Interviewee:** Jenny Zhang (Owner) and/or GEP Intern  
**Purpose:** Clarify the real problem(s), define MVP scope, prioritise requirements (Website + Odoo), and align on success criteria for the next sprints.

---

## 1) Current Situation & Biggest Pain
1. If you had to describe the *single biggest problem* we should solve in this project, what would it be?
2. What costs you the most time during a normal week (website/inquiries, orders, stock, customer communication, admin work)?
3. Which parts of your workflow feel “manual” or error-prone today?
4. What is currently working well and should **not** be changed?

---

## 2) Goals & Success Criteria (Measurable)
5. At the end of this project (May 30), what would make you say: “This project was successful”?
6. Which 3 outcomes are most important to you (rank them):  
   - more incoming inquiries  
   - better trust/professional image  
   - easier customer communication  
   - faster internal operations  
   - fewer errors / better overview  
   - other: ________
7. How would you like to measure improvement? (e.g., fewer emails/calls, faster order handling, more inquiries, clearer overview)
8. Are there any “must-have” deadlines (e.g., trade fairs, seasonal peaks, customer visits)?

---

## 3) Website — Target Audience & Content Needs
9. Who is the primary audience for the website? (B2B buyers, wholesalers, supermarkets, restaurants, import/export partners)
10. What is the #1 action you want visitors to take? (call, email, inquiry form, request quotation, download product list)
11. What information do potential customers ask for most often before doing business with you?
12. Which trust signals are important for you? (company story, certifications, photos, location at Fresh Park, trade partners, testimonials)
13. Which languages do you need on the website (EN/NL/DE/Chinese)? Which one is most important?
14. What content do you already have available? (logo, images, product descriptions, company text, certificates)
15. Who should maintain the website content after we deliver it?

---

## 4) Website — Functionality & MVP Scope
16. Which pages are essential for MVP? (e.g., Home, About, Products, Contact, Logistics/Quality, FAQ)
17. Do you need an inquiry form? If yes: what fields are required and what should happen after submission?
18. What is the preferred contact flow: email only, phone, WhatsApp, or a structured inquiry pipeline?
19. Do you need a product catalogue on the website (simple list) or not at MVP stage?
20. Are there any legal requirements we must include? (privacy policy, cookie banner, terms, imprint)

---

## 5) Internal System (Odoo) — What Exactly Should Improve?
21. Which Odoo parts do you currently use (or want to use)? (CRM, Sales, Inventory, Purchase, Accounting, Email templates)
22. What are the top 3 frustrations in the current system?
23. Which internal tasks take too many clicks / too many tools / too much manual copy-paste?
24. Which data do you frequently search for but cannot find quickly?
25. Where do mistakes happen most often (stock, customer details, invoices, delivery dates)?
26. If we improve only **one** workflow in Odoo during this project, which one should it be?

---

## 6) Process & Workflow Clarification (AS-IS → TO-BE)
27. Can you walk us through an end-to-end process you want improved? (from inquiry → order → delivery → follow-up)
28. Who performs each step today (you vs. intern vs. others)?
29. Where does information get lost or duplicated?
30. Which parts should become more standardised (templates, checklists, statuses, approvals)?

---

## 7) Users, Roles, Permissions & Security
31. Who will use the website backend/CMS? Who will use Odoo?
32. Do different people need different access rights? (view-only vs editing)
33. Are there any privacy/security constraints we must follow? (customer data, supplier data, internal pricing)
34. Do you need logging/traceability (who changed what, when)?

---

## 8) Constraints & Dependencies
35. What access can we get and when? (current website platform, Odoo environment, admin accounts)
36. Are there external IT providers involved? If yes: what can we change ourselves and what needs their approval?
37. Are there technical constraints we must respect? (hosting, domain, existing tools)
38. What risks do you see for this project? (time, access, unclear priorities, content availability)

---

## 9) Prioritisation (MVP vs Nice-to-Have)
39. From everything discussed, what are the **top 5 must-haves** for the next 4 weeks?
40. What would you consider “nice-to-have” but not required?
41. If we need to cut scope, what should we cut first without harming your core goals?

---

## 10) Validation & Next Steps
42. Can we summarise the problem statement in one sentence and confirm it with you?
43. Can we confirm the MVP scope as a short checklist and get your approval?
44. How often should we validate progress with you/intern (weekly review, short feedback loops)?
45. Is there anything we didn’t ask that you consider important?
