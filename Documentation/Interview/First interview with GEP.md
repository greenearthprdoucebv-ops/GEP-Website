# Interview Confirmation – Green Earth Produce

**Participants:** Mhd Eslam Raayn, Jenny Zhang

**Date:** March 3, 2026 

 
-----

## Overview

This document confirms the interview held with **Green Earth Produce (GEP)** and outlines what was agreed upon during the meeting.

-----

## What Was Agreed

Green Earth Produce has agreed to work with us as our **Project provider**.

During the interview, GEP shared that they are looking for help in two main areas:

- **Improving their online presence** – making it easier for people to find and connect with them online.
- **Improving their internal system** – updating the tools they use to manage their day-to-day work.

-----

## How We Will Work Together

To make sure things run smoothly, both sides agreed on the following:

- **Jenny Zhang**, the owner of Green Earth Produce, will oversee our work and will be available for **30 minutes each week** online to give feedback and guidance.
- GEP will provide us with an **intern** who will help share Jenny’s ideas and opinions throughout the project. The intern will act as the official representative of Green Earth Produce.

-----

## Next Steps

Both parties are aligned and ready to move forward. This document serves as a record of the interview and the agreements made.
