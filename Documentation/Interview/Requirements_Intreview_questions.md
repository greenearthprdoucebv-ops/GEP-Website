# Requirements Elicitation Questions – GreenEarth Produce Website

## 1. Company Overview

1. What are the main activities of GreenEarth Produce?
2. Who are your main customers or target markets?
3. What products do you mainly sell (e.g., ginger, vegetables)?

---

## 2. Business Goals

4. What is the main goal of creating the new website?
5. What problems do you currently experience without a professional website?
6. How do customers currently contact your company?

---

## 3. Website Content

7. What information should be presented on the website?
8. Do you want to display product pages for each product?
9. Should the website include company information such as history, mission, or certifications?

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

10. Should customers be able to contact you through a contact form?
11. Do you want the ability to update products or information yourself?
12. Should the website support multiple languages?

---

## 5. User Experience

13. Who are the main users of the website (buyers, partners, distributors)?
14. What kind of impression should the website give (professional, modern, minimal)?
15. Are there websites you like as inspiration?

---

## 6. Technical Questions

16. Do you already have a domain and hosting?
17. Does the website need to integrate with your current Odoo system?
18. Are there any security or data protection requirements?

---

## 7. Maintenance

19. Who will maintain or update the website after it is finished?
20. How often will the website content need to change?
