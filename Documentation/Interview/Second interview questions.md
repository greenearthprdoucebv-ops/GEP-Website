# Initial Interview Questions – Green Earth Produce

**Interviewee:** Jenny Zhang (Owner)
**Purpose:** Initial meeting to understand GEP's business, systems, and expectations

---

## About the Business

1. Can you walk us through a typical order — from the moment you receive it to the moment it goes out the door?
2. Who are your most frequent or biggest customers?
3. Who do you consider your biggest competitor, and what do they do better than you right now?

---

## About Marketing

4. How do you currently promote GEP? (social media, word of mouth, ads, etc.)
5. Do you have anyone handling marketing, or is it something you manage yourself?

---

## About the Website

6. Do you currently have a website? If yes, what do you like and dislike about it?
7. What do you want people to do when they land on your website? (place orders, learn about you, contact you?)
8. Are there any websites — yours or competitors — that you really like the look or feel of?

---

## About the Internal System

9. What tools or software do you currently use to manage orders, stock, or day-to-day work?
10. What is the biggest frustration with how things are managed right now?
11. What would the perfect system look like for you and your team?

---

## About IT

12. How many IT staff do you have in-house, if any?
13. Do you work with an external IT company? If yes, what do they handle for you?
14. Would it be possible to see the contract or scope of work with that IT company?
15. What are you expecting from an external IT partner — what does good support look like to you?

---

## Wrapping Up

16. Is there anything about GEP that you think we should know before we start?
17. What does success look like for you at the end of this project?

---

## Current Online Footprint

18. When someone searches for Green Earth Produce online, what do you want them to find?
19. Are you listed on platforms like Google Business, LinkedIn, Alibaba, or wholesale directories?
20. Do customers ever find you through Google or online searches?
21. Do you track where new customers come from?
22.Do you receive inquiries through email or website forms?

---

## Target Audience Online

23. Who should the website primarily target — wholesalers, supermarkets, restaurants, or distributors?
24. Are your customers mostly in the Netherlands or across Europe?
25. Do potential customers usually already know you before visiting the website?
26. What information do customers typically ask for before placing an order?
