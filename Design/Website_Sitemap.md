# Website Sitemap – GreenEarth Produce

## 1. Introduction

The sitemap defines the structure of the GreenEarth Produce website.  
It represents the organization of pages and how users navigate through the website.

The goal is to ensure that visitors can easily find information about the company, its products, and how to contact the business.

---

## 2. Website Structure

Home
│
├── About Us
│   ├── Company Overview
│   ├── Mission & Vision
│   └── Certifications
│
├── Products
│   ├── Ginger
│   ├── Other Products
│   └── Product Details
│
├── Trade & Logistics
│   ├── Export Information
│   └── Supply Chain
│
├── News / Updates
│
└── Contact
    ├── Contact Form
    └── Company Location

---

## 3. Navigation Concept

The website will include a main navigation menu that allows users to access the primary sections:

- Home
- About Us
- Products
- Trade & Logistics
- News
- Contact

This structure ensures that users can quickly access product information and contact the company.

---

## 4. Design Considerations

The sitemap focuses on:

- Clear navigation
- Simple information structure
- Easy access to product information
- Quick contact with the company
