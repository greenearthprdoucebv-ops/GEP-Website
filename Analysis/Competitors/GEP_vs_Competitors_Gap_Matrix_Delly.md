## 1) Benchmark Dimensions (0–5)

Each competitor is rated on 6 dimensions (0–5). Total = /30.

1. **Website content depth** (products, story, buyer info)
2. **Conversion readiness** (CTA, inquiry flow, contact clarity)
3. **Trust signals** (team, certifications, legal pages, transparency)
4. **SEO basics (visible signals)** (structured pages, indexable content, local intent)
5. **Social media / B2B activity** (LinkedIn/Instagram/Facebook)
6. **Reputation signals** (ratings/reviews present and credible)

## 7) Actionable Requirements for GEP (derived from benchmarks)

> These align with the gaps identified in GEP’s current online presence analysis (very basic website + low discoverability).  [oai_citation:62‡GEP Company’s online presence analysis.md](sediment://file_00000000f0a07243a72ee062131d3daf)

1. Add **product pages / product categories** with clear buyer info (origin, seasonality, packaging, contact).
2. Add a **strong trust layer**: company story, location (Fresh Park), team/contact persons, certifications if available.
3. Implement a **conversion-focused inquiry flow**: contact form + clear CTA + response expectations.
4. Improve **local discoverability**: Google Business Profile + consistent NAP (name/address/phone) across channels.
5. Establish a **B2B LinkedIn baseline**: consistent branding + regular posts (product updates, availability, trade fairs).
