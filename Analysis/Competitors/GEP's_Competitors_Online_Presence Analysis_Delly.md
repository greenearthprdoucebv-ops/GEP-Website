2. Add a **strong trust layer**: company story, location (Fresh Park), team/contact persons, certifications if available.
3. Implement a **conversion-focused inquiry flow**: contact form + clear CTA + response expectations.
4. Improve **local discoverability**: Google Business Profile + consistent NAP (name/address/phone) across channels.
5. Establish a **B2B LinkedIn baseline**: consistent branding + regular posts (product updates, availability, trade fairs).

## 2) GEP — Current Online Presence (AS-IS)

### 2.1 Website visibility
- At the moment, GEP does **not appear to have a clearly discoverable official company website** (or it is not easily findable through typical searches).  
- The public online footprint is mostly represented by **directory entries** and **business listing pages** (e.g., company directories and yellow pages).  [oai_citation:53‡Companyinfo](https://companyinfo.nl/organisatieprofiel/groothandel-in-groenten-en-fruit/green-earth-produce-b-v-venlo-74852264-000042778557?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
- This aligns with our internal analysis that GEP currently has **low discoverability and only a basic online presence**, which is a barrier for B2B trust and lead generation.  [oai_citation:54‡GEP Company’s online presence analysis.md](sediment://file_00000000f0a07243a72ee062131d3daf)  

### 2.2 Company listings & contact availability
- GEP is listed with business information such as address and industry classification (wholesale fruit & vegetables) in company directories.  [oai_citation:55‡Companyinfo](https://companyinfo.nl/organisatieprofiel/groothandel-in-groenten-en-fruit/green-earth-produce-b-v-venlo-74852264-000042778557?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
- Such listings help basic verification (“the company exists”), but they do not replace a professional website that communicates products, services, and trust signals.

### 2.3 Social presence
- A LinkedIn profile for “Green Earth Produce B.V.” is discoverable, indicating at least minimal B2B presence.  [oai_citation:56‡LinkedIn](https://nl.linkedin.com/in/green-earth-produce-b-v-35a570371?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
- However, without a strong website, LinkedIn alone is not enough to communicate product assortment, service level, and clear inquiry flows.

### 2.4 Content & conversion readiness (current state)
Based on our earlier internal online presence assessment:  [oai_citation:57‡GEP Company’s online presence analysis.md](sediment://file_00000000f0a07243a72ee062131d3daf)  
- Product information is currently not presented in a structured, buyer-friendly way online (e.g., product categories, seasonality, origin, packaging, inquiry context).
- There is no clearly optimized lead capture path (inquiry form / CTA structure).
- Trust signals (team, quality standards/certifications, story, location proof) are not visible enough.

**AS-IS conclusion:**  
GEP’s current online presence does not support a typical B2B buyer journey (discover → evaluate → trust → inquire). This creates a strong justification for the project’s website focus and structured improvements.
