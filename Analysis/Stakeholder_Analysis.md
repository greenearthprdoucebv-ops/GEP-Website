# Stakeholder Analysis – GreenEarth Produce Website Project

## 1. Introduction

A stakeholder analysis identifies the individuals or groups that influence or are affected by a system being developed.  
For the GreenEarth Produce project, the system is a professional company website that will represent the business online and support communication with potential buyers.

The purpose of this analysis is to identify the key stakeholders, understand their interests and expectations, and evaluate their level of influence on the project. This helps ensure that the website meets both business goals and user needs.

---

## 2. Identified Stakeholders

The following stakeholders were identified for the GreenEarth Produce website project:

- Company Owner / Management  
- Company Employees  
- Potential Customers / Buyers  
- Suppliers  
- Logistics Partners  
- Website Visitors  
- Development Team  
- University Supervisor

---

## 3. Stakeholder Overview

| Stakeholder | Role | Interests / Goals | Influence |
|--------------|------|-------------------|-----------|
| Company Owner / Management | Main decision maker and project sponsor | Wants a professional website that increases visibility, represents the company, and attracts new buyers | High |
| Company Employees | May maintain product information and communicate with clients | Want an easy way to update information and manage contact requests | Medium |
| Customers / Buyers | Businesses interested in purchasing products | Want clear product information and an easy way to contact the company | Medium |
| Suppliers | Provide agricultural products to the company | Want the company to grow and increase sales | Low |
| Logistics Partners | Responsible for transporting goods | Need clear communication with the company regarding logistics and operations | Low |
| Website Visitors | Potential customers exploring the website | Want clear information, fast navigation, and a professional appearance | Medium |
| Development Team | Students responsible for building the website | Want clear requirements and successful project completion | High |
| University Supervisor | Oversees and evaluates the project | Wants the project to demonstrate proper analysis, design, and implementation | Medium |

---

## 4. Stakeholder Influence and Interest

Stakeholders can also be classified based on their level of influence and interest in the project.

**High Influence**
- Company Owner / Management
- Development Team

**Medium Influence**
- Company Employees
- Customers / Buyers
- University Supervisor

**Low Influence**
- Suppliers
- Logistics Partners
- Website Visitors

The company owner has the highest influence since they define the business goals and approve the final system. Customers and website visitors are also important because the website must satisfy their needs for information and usability.

---

## 5. Conclusion

The stakeholder analysis shows that the most important stakeholders for this project are the company owner, the development team, and the potential customers. These stakeholders will strongly influence the requirements, design decisions, and overall success of the website.

Understanding their needs early in the project will help ensure that the final website effectively supports the business objectives of GreenEarth Produce.
