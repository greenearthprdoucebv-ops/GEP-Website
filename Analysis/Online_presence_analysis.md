## Recommendations

1. **Rebuild the website** – The current site is not fit for purpose. A proper website with products, contact info, about page, and clear calls to action is essential.
1. **Set up a Google Business Profile** – Free, quick, and immediately improves local search visibility.
1. **Improve LinkedIn** – Grow the follower base and create more consistent, visually appealing content.
1. **Consider Instagram** – Useful for product showcase, especially for a fresh food company with visual products.
1. **Review Odoo setup** – Since GEP already uses Odoo, the focus should be on optimising and extending their current system rather than replacing it.
