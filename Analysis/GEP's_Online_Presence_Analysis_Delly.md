## 5) Key Problems (AS-IS Summary)
**Problem 1 — Low discoverability & weak first impression**  
A website exists, but it is too basic and provides little to no persuasive information for B2B buyers.  [oai_citation:21‡GEP's online presence analysis.md](sediment://file_0000000027407246a47fa024c9e59293)  

**Problem 2 — Missing product communication**  
Without product categories and buyer information, potential customers cannot quickly assess whether GEP supplies what they need.   

**Problem 3 — No strong inquiry/conversion flow**  
The website does not support a structured lead capture process, which reduces inquiries and increases manual work.  [oai_citation:22‡GEP Company’s online presence analysis.md](sediment://file_00000000f0a07243a72ee062131d3daf)  

**Problem 4 — Trust signals are not visible enough**  
Competitors commonly show certifications/quality, team transparency, and clear company story. GEP currently does not.
